Images

This page covers common images formats, but you may also be looking for links or useful extras!

JPGs

Pros

  • JPG images store large color palletes in a relatively small file size.

Cons

  • JPG images cannot store transparent pixels.

GIFs

Pros

  • GIF images are good for animation.

Cons

  • GIF images have a limited color palette.
  • Transparents is either an on or off option.

PNGs

Pros

  • PNG iamges are good for diagrams, icons, or anything that's not a photo or animated.
  • No color palette or transparency limitations.

Cons

  • PNG images of an equivalent quality to a JPG are bigger.

SVGs

Pros

  • SVGs are vector based as opposed to the pixel based images above.
  • SVGs can scale up or down without any loss of quality (GREAT for responsive design!).

Cons

  • Lots of text can have a big impact on file size!
