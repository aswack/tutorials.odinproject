Links

This page covers the types of links, but you may also be looking for images or useful extras!

There are three links of links:

  • Absolute links: like to Mozilla Developer Netork, which is an excellent resource for web developers.
  • Relative links: like to our own extras page, which resides on a different folder in the server.
  • Root-relative links: like to the home page of our website, but those aren't useful to us right this moment (but will be in the future!)
